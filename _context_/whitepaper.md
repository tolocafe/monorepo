# Tolo — Company Whitepaper

## What is Tolo

Tolo is a coffee shop brand and coffee roaster, currently operating two locations in Mexico State.

## Products

- **Physical locations**: two cafés in Mexico State
- **Mobile app** (iOS & Android): ordering, menu browsing, rewards, e-wallet, location finder
- **Website** (tolo.cafe): marketing and e-commerce
- **Poster POS plugin**: displays customer information to baristas

## Target Audience

- Local regulars
- Specialty coffee enthusiasts
- Quality seekers

## Team

Solo project.

## Vision

- Expand to more locations and estates
- Build the strongest online wholesale coffee brand for people and businesses
